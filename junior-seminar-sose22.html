Junior Seminar Geometry and Dynamics

(or: Pizza Seminar)


The idea of this seminar is to bring together all PhD students of the research station to talk about math (and other things) in a nice atmosphere. We will have short (20-30 minutes) math talks where everyone can talk about what they are working on, what they found interesting recently, or anything that is cool math. We will bring pizza for everyone!
