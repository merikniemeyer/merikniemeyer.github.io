About me

I am a PhD student in Anna Wienhard's Geometry, Groups and Dynamics division at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences in Leipzig. I started my PhD in 2021 at Heidelberg University before moving to Leipzig in October 2023. Previously, I got my master's degree in Theoretical and Mathematical Physics at LMU Munich.

My research is focused on non-commutative cluster algebras and their connection to higher Teichmüller theory, specifically on cluster coordinates for Theta-positive representations and the investigation of the underlying cluster structures. More generally, I am interested in many aspects of higher Teichmüller theory and cluster combinatorics.
