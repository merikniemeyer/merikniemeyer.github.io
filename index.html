About me

Welcome to my web page! I am a PhD student in Anna Wienhard's Differential Geometry research group at Heidelberg University. Previously, I got my master's degree in Theoretical and Mathematical Physics at LMU Munich.

Currently, I am interested in cluster algebras, Fock-Goncharov coordinates, Theta-positivity, and representations into SO(p,q). More generally, I want to learn more about higher Teichmüller theory and Anosov representations.

Last semester I co-organised some seminars, all of which can be found under activities. There you will also find news for this semester soon.


News

  • I just returned from a visit to my doctoral adviser Anna Wienhard at the Institute for Advanced Study in Princeton. In the USA, I also had the opportunity to visit Christian Zickert at the University of Maryland and Daniele Alessandrini at Columbia University.
  • In October I gave a talk about Theta positivity as part of the Arbeitsgemeinschaft on Higher Rank Teichmüller Theory at the MFO.
